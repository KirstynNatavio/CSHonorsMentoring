Our Approach

Does it work?

Yup! Big time! Our first cohort of mentees at CCNY are working this summer at RBC, Barclays, and Centiment. And these are our FRESHMEN!!

Getting a job is hard work!

We know what it takes to succeed. We prep our students from Day 1. Before they even start their academics or think of looking for a job, never mind a career.

Our scholars must prove their value and worth. They must commit to give us their time and tenacity.

In turn, we commit to build their professional outlook. Break down barriers. Open doors to their future.

The Future

We know we are a stealth school. But we are in Tech Alley’s own backyard! Our home is CCNY 137th Street, in Harlem USA. We have a 140 year history of elevating the ranks of New York’s immigrants and first generation scholars.

Born out of Necessity

CCNY Code is a side project built out of duty and honor in February 2017 as an accident. A chance meeting between a CS student and a single alumnus Graduates of CCNY. Separated by disciplines, one a budding CS engineer, the other an ex-journalist and editor turned lawyer and asset manager. Separated by 40 years, David Wysoki and Anthony Shalagin met on campus after an event and realized they desired to do something spectacular. CCNY Code is their invention.
