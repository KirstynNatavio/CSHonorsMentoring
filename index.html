Getting a job is hard work!


Getting a great job is even harder.

That’s where Code is Life steps in.

We know what it takes to succeed.

We prep our students from Day 1.

Before they even start their academics.

Before they even think of looking for a job, never mind a career.

We demand our scholars commit to a code of conduct.

They must prove their value and worth.

They must commit to elevate their reach.

To give us their time and tenacity.

In turn, we commit to build their professional outlook.

Break down barriers. Open doors to their future.

We expect that true success will take four years to measure, when they graduate.

But we have already seen signs of success.

Born out of Necessity


Code Is Life is a side project built out of duty and honor.

It started as an accident.

A chance meeting between a CS student and a single alumnus Graduates of CCNY.

Separated by disciplines, one a budding CS engineer, the other an ex-journalist and editor turned lawyer and asset manager.

Separated by 40 years, David Wysoki and Anthony Shalagin met on campus after an event and realized they desired to do something spectacular.

Code Is Life is their invention.
